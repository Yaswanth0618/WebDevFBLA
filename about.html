Our Journey

January 1, 1970
Founding and Opening
+ -

John Smith established the first Culinary Harbor restaurant in Alpharetta, Georgia, with a modest $100 and the unwavering support of his family and friends. The grand opening was a significant milestone, marking the beginning of John's dream to create a unique culinary experience. The initial menu featured innovative dishes that combined traditional flavors with a modern twist, quickly gaining the attention of local food enthusiasts. The restaurant's warm ambiance and exceptional service set the tone for what would become a hallmark of the Culinary Harbor brand.

April 24, 1982
Expansion and Opening of New Locations
+ -

Buoyed by the success of the Alpharetta location, John decided to expand Culinary Harbor by opening additional branches in neighboring cities. The first expansion was met with enthusiastic responses, prompting John to consider national and eventually international growth. Each new location maintained the same high standards of quality and service that characterized the original restaurant. This strategic expansion allowed Culinary Harbor to reach a wider audience, solidifying its reputation as a premier dining destination.

July 30, 1989
10th Location
+ -

Reaching the milestone of the 100th Culinary Harbor location was a momentous occasion for John and his team. This achievement was celebrated with a grand event that included loyal customers, employees, and local dignitaries. The 100th restaurant embodied the brand’s evolution while staying true to its core values of innovation and exceptional dining experiences. This milestone underscored the hard work, dedication, and vision that propelled Culinary Harbor from a single restaurant to a global chain.

December 21, 1999
Launch of Franchise Program
+ -

To accelerate growth, John launched the Culinary Harbor franchise program, allowing entrepreneurs to open their own branches under the established brand. This initiative provided comprehensive training and support to ensure each franchise maintained Culinary Harbor’s high standards. The franchise model quickly attracted interest, resulting in a significant increase in the number of locations. By empowering others to join the Culinary Harbor family, John expanded the brand’s reach and created new business opportunities.

October 16, 2007
Best Innovative Restaurant Chain
+ -

Culinary Harbor received the prestigious award for Best Innovative Restaurant Chain, recognizing its commitment to culinary excellence and creative dining concepts. This accolade was a testament to the hard work and innovation of John and his team, who continually pushed the boundaries of traditional dining. The award highlighted Culinary Harbor’s role as a trendsetter in the industry, inspiring other restaurants to innovate and elevate their offerings.
