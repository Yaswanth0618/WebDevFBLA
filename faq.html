Frequently Asked Questions

  • What type of cuisine does Culinary Harbor specialize in?
    Culinary Harbor specializes in American, African, Chinese, French, Indian, Japanese & Thai Cuisine.
  • How can I apply for a job at Culinary Harbor?
    Visit our "Careers" page to learn more and click on the "Apply" button to submit your application online.
  • Are reservations required, and how can I make one?
    While reservations are not required, we recommend making a reservation for a better dining experience. You can make reservations by calling us.
  • What are the benefits of working at Culinary Harbor?
    Explore the "Benefits" page for information on employee benefits and reasons to join our team.
  • Does Culinary Harbor offer catering services?
    Yes, we offer catering services. Please call us for pricing and details on our offerings.
  • Are there any ongoing promotions or special discounts?
    There aren't any available right now.
  • How can I leave a review for Culinary Harbor?
    You can share your feedback on Yelp or Google Reviews.
  • Is Culinary Harbor accessible for individuals with dietary restrictions?
    Yes, we strive to accommodate various dietary preferences and restrictions. Feel free to inform our staff, and they will assist you accordingly.
  • What are the accepted payment methods at Culinary Harbor?
    We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, cash, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum.
  • Does Culinary Harbor host events or private parties?
    Yes, Culinary Harbor is available for events and private parties.
  • How can I contact Culinary Harbor for general inquiries?
    You can reach us through the contact details provided on the footer of each page.
